UWC ISAK Cafeteria Guidelines

Please take a little time to read the following document. The document is about some
guidelines we would like you to follow while using the School Cafeteria.

Meal Schedules
Monday to Friday
Breakfast 7:15-7:55
Lunch 12:15-13:00
Dinner 18:00-18:40
Snack 10:45

Saturday, Sunday, and School Holidays
Breakfast 8:30-9:30
Lunch 12:20-13:20
Dinner 18:00-18:40
Snack 15:00

Please come on time to every meal and return your trays and dishes before the end of the
meal schedule.

Please keep your School Cafeteria clean for yourself and for the rest of the community. We will
provide you towels when needed.

Wear a Mask and sanitize your hands when you come to the cafeteria.

When serving yourself, please serve yourself in a balanced way. Please serve yourself
vegetables, which are a very important part of your daily nutrition, and not just the main or
rice or potatoes.

When you come back to serve yourself some seconds, or refill your drinks, please wear a mask.
During Lunch time you can choose between either the main dish of the day or the noodle of
the day or the lunch special (Monday to Friday). You can serve yourself the side dishes with
any of these options.

We prepare Vegetarian and Halal food according to the numbers of Vegetarian and Halal
People. If you are not either Vegetarian or Halal, please refrain from asking for these dishes
until we can make sure we are going to have enough food to serve all of them.

If you have any dietary restriction (Food Allergy, Vegetarian, Halal, etc), please be very careful
to check the information in the Menu Cards every time. If you are not sure, please ask our
staff.

Please follow the Limit Cards.

Please throw eggshells in the garbage bin. Also, if you drop plates, cups, cutlery etc. inside the
food waste disposing area, please let our staff know. The food waste collected will go to a
composting machine and this machine will break if eggshells, paper, plastic, or anything else
besides food waste enters it. Repairing the machine costs the school a big amount of money.
Let’s all take care of it!

The food served during Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner is ONLY to be eaten inside the cafeteria.
No food can be taken out by students unless it’s requested by a school Faculty or Staff.
No Plates, Bowls, Cups and Cutlery can be taken to the houses. They belong to the cafeteria.
Also please don’t use them for other activities such as science experiments, art, etc.
Don’t stick chewing gum on anything inside the cafeteria including plates, trays, bowls, table,
walls, etc. Please throw in the trash.

When serving yourself something to drink please use only one cup every meal.

Please don’t bring personal items (plates, bowls, etc.) to the dishwashing area.

During weekends and school holidays, we will prepare bentos only for events organized by the
school. If any student will need bento for any project or event related to the school, please
request the bento through a Faculty or Staff (At least one week ahead) mentioning the event,
date, time and any special dietary policy like allergies, halal, etc.

We have a café shop where we sell home baked snacks and many delicious things. Here you
can pay by cash or cashless, using a personal account system where you can charge your
account by cash or remotely by PayPal. Every student has his/her own PIN Number. Please
feel free to pass by the cafeteria and ask Dario (Cezars Kitchen Manager) for your number.
Never share this information with anyone else.

When planning some event that is related to the cafeteria or any meal please communicate
before the 15th of the previous month as the latest (if possible, 2 months ahead) through a
school Faculty or Staff.

There is a communication book where you can give us your feedback. Please use to
communicate with us.

Thank you!

Dario Yasuda

Kitchen Manager of Cezars Kitchen UWC ISAK
